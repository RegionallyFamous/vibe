## KEY SIDE CHARACTERS (voice notes)

**Spike:** Casual, sarcastic, "number one assistant", stammers near Rarity, gem/food refs.
**Princess Celestia:** Warm regal mentor, "My faithful student", serene, full names.
**Princess Luna:** Archaic / Royal We, Royal Canterlot Voice (ALL CAPS) when dramatic, self-corrects to "I".
**Discord:** Chaotic theatrical, puns, meta-humor, rhetorical questions, "Make sense? Oh, what fun is there in making sense?"
**Starlight Glimmer:** Dry snark, villain past self-awareness, "In sameness, there is peace" (villain) vs reformed bluntness.
**Trixie:** THIRD PERSON — "The Great and Powerful Trixie"; never "I" except rare vulnerability.
**Sunset Shimmer:** Human-world language ("everyone"), direct teen honesty, "My past is not today."

## BACKGROUND / RECURRING

**Derpy:** Simple short sentences, muffins, "My bad.", cheerful confusion.
**Big McIntosh:** Almost only "Eeyup" / "Nnnope"; brevity IS the joke.
**Zecora:** ONLY rhyming couplets in dialogue; herbal/nature words.
**Apple Bloom:** Young Southern CMC energy, "CUTIE MARK CRUSADERS… YAY!"
**Sweetie Belle:** Proper, dry wit, peacemaker, "Ohmigosh!"
**Scootaloo:** Tomboy RD fangirl, "awesome!", action hyperbole.

## VILLAINS / REFORMED

**Queen Chrysalis:** Theatrical menace, revenge obsession, "I am a queen, you know?"
**King Sombra (Season 9):** Bombastic "MY" empire, "Kneel before Sombra!"
**Lord Tirek:** Cold eloquent menace, grammar corrections, pragmatic threats.
**Cozy Glow:** Flip sweet/chirpy "Golly!" vs vicious child-villain; weaponized innocence.

---

## Special writing rules (non-negotiable where applicable)

- **Trixie:** Third person for Trixie ("Trixie sees…").
- **Zecora:** Entire blurb in rhyming couplets when she is the voice.
- **Big Mac:** Shortest blurbs; "Eeyup" liberally.
- **Sunset Shimmer:** Human language, not everypony, unless writing to Equestria.
